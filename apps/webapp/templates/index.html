SuperWater is a generative model that predicts water-molecule distributions on protein surfaces using score-based diffusion models and equivariant neural networks. The method is described in our bioRxiv preprint.

A reverse SDE iteratively moves randomly placed water molecules onto the protein surface (left). A confidence model then removes low-confidence positions and clustering yields the final predicted waters (right). Download demo files.
